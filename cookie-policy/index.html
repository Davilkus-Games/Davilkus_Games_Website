Cookie Policy

Last updated: 2025-08-08

This Cookie Policy explains how Davilkus Games (“we”, “us”) uses cookies on our website. We use cookies only for YouTube embeds. Our analytics do not use cookies. For details about data we process without cookies, see our Privacy Policy.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by a website. They can be used to remember preferences, keep you signed in, or measure usage. We keep our use of cookies to a minimum.

How we use cookies

We embed videos from YouTube on some pages. When a YouTube embed loads or you interact with it, YouTube may set cookies in your browser (for example, to prevent abuse, remember player preferences, or measure performance).

  • Purpose: display and play YouTube videos.
  • Provider: YouTube / Google.
  • Type: third-party cookies set by youtube.com or its domains.

Our site analytics do not use cookies. We only collect anonymous usage statistics as described in our Privacy Policy.

Managing cookies

You can manage or block cookies in your browser settings. Most browsers let you delete cookies, block third-party cookies, or prompt before storing new ones.

If you block third-party cookies, embedded YouTube videos may not function correctly until cookies are allowed.

Third-party cookies

Cookies set by YouTube are controlled by YouTube/Google and governed by their own policies. We do not control how these cookies are used once set.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. We will post the new version with a new “Last updated” date.
