Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-08-08

This Privacy Policy explains how Davilkus Games handles information on our website. We do not create user accounts and we do not collect personal information, except limited anonymous analytics as described below.

Scope

This policy applies to davilkusgames.com and any pages we host under this domain. It does not cover third-party platforms where our games are distributed (e.g., Google Play, itch.io, Yandex Games) or any services they operate.

Information We Collect

We collect anonymous usage statistics to understand traffic and improve our site. This includes your IP address processed only to derive non-precise location (country) and to maintain basic fraud/abuse prevention. We do not build profiles and do not track you across sites.

  • Pages visited, referrer (if any), timestamps, and basic device information (e.g., screen size, browser).
  • IP address (used to infer country-level location and for aggregate statistics).

We do not collect names, emails, payment data, or precise location through our website. If you choose to email us, your message will include your email address by nature of email itself, and we will use it only to reply.

Legal Basis (EEA/UK)

Where GDPR or similar laws apply, our legal basis for processing analytics data is our legitimate interests in operating, securing, and improving our website, balanced against your privacy. You can opt out of optional analytics in your browser or via our Cookie Policy controls when applicable.

How We Use Information

  • Measure site performance and popularity of pages.
  • Detect and prevent abuse (e.g., traffic spikes from bots).
  • Plan content and improvements for our community.

Retention

Analytics records are kept only as long as necessary for aggregate reporting and security. We do not attempt to re-identify individuals.

Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your data. We may share anonymous, aggregated insights (e.g., visits by country) publicly.

We may disclose information if required by law or to respond to valid legal requests, or to protect our rights, users, or the public.

Your Rights

Depending on your location (e.g., EEA/UK/California), you may have rights to access, delete, or object to processing of your personal data. Because we do not maintain user accounts or identify you, our ability to act on requests may be limited. You can still contact us and we’ll do our best within the technical constraints.

To exercise rights or ask questions, email contact@davilkusgames.com.

Children’s Privacy

Our website is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through this site.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect our site and the limited analytics we process. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Third-Party Services

Our site may link to third-party platforms (e.g., Discord, YouTube, Telegram) or storefronts (e.g., Google Play, itch.io). Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, contact us at contact@davilkusgames.com or support@davilkusgames.com.
